5 Minute S'mores Cereal Bars

This delightfully easy recipe for 5 Minute S'mores Cereal Bars is going to become a new favorite for sure! Just 5 ingredients and loads of authentic s'mores flavor, these bars are adored by kids and adults alike!

Course Dessert Cuisine American Keyword 5 minute s'mores cereal bars, cereal bars Prep Time 3 minutes minutes Cook Time 2 minutes minutes Total Time 5 minutes minutes Servings 16 bars Calories 211kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup corn syrup
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 cups graham cracker cereal
  • 3 cups miniature marshmallows divided

Instructions

  • Line an 8-inch or 9-inch baking pan with non-stick foil. Set aside.

  • Combine 2 cups of marshmallows and cereal in a large, heat-safe bowl.

  • Pour corn syrup into a microwave-safe bowl container. I used a glass Pyrex measuring cup.

  • Microwave corn syrup for 1 minute on high heat.

  • Stir in chocolate chips until melted.

  • Quickly stir in vanilla extract.

  • Pour chocolate mixture over the top of the cereal and marshmallows and stir until coated.

  • Scoop contents into the prepared baking dish and press down with the bottom of a measuring cup or large spoon.

  • Refrigerate for a few minutes until the chocolate has set up. Top with remaining cup of marshmallows and place under broiler for 1-2 minutes or until golden brown.

  • Cut into squares and serve.
